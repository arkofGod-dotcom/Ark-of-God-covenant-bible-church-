Vision and Mission

👁
Our Vision

A People Ready for the Kings Return

The Ark of God Covenant Faith Bible Ministry exists to be a divine habitation, a holy ark, where God's glory dwells, His people are transformed, and the nations are reached with the uncompromising Word of the Living God. We are called to raise a consecrated generation, fully awakened to their prophetic purpose, walking in covenant faith, and prepared in spirit, soul, and body for the imminent Second Coming of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.

Like Noah who built the ark at God's command and preserved a remnant for a new world, we are building a spiritual ark, gathering, discipling, and readying men and women of all nations to stand before the Son of Man without shame. We carry a burning mandate to raise kingdom men and women who will advance the dominion of God in every sphere of life, every city, every nation, until the whole earth is filled with His glory.

We will not be silent. We will not be still. We will preach, we will disciple, we will send, until He comes.

🎯
Our Mission

Building Kingdom Architects for an Eternal Purpose

Our mission is to aggressively pursue the Great Commission by preaching the unadulterated Gospel of Jesus Christ, establishing believers in sound doctrine, and raising kingdom architects who will transform communities, nations, and generations.

Through Spirit-empowered worship, prophetic teaching, covenant prayer, and intentional discipleship, we are committed to winning souls with urgency and compassion, knowing the time is short. Building disciples who are rooted in the Word and filled with the Spirit. Raising leaders who carry kingdom authority into every sphere of influence. Preparing a Bride that is spotless, watchful, and ready for the Bridegroom's return. Demonstrating God's power through signs, wonders, and transformative community impact.

We are not merely building a church. We are building a covenant people, a holy remnant, who will stand unashamed at the appearing of our great God and Saviour, Jesus Christ.

Leadership

Meet Our Founder and Senior Pastor

Servant of God - Covenant Apostle - Teacher of the Word

Pastor Dr E.A Abioye

In the vast tapestry of God's redemptive story, there emerge men and women who are not merely products of their time, but who are divinely manufactured vessels of a higher purpose, individuals through whom heaven bends low and touches the earth. Pastor Dr E.A Abioye is unquestionably one of such men.

Born of the Spirit, refined in the furnace of affliction, and anointed with a fire that neither circumstances nor opposition could extinguish, Pastor Abioye emerged onto the kingdom landscape with a mandate that is as clear as it is consuming: to open the Ark of God's Covenant to a generation desperately in need of divine cover, eternal truth, and kingdom purpose.

He is the visionary founder and Senior Pastor of The Ark of God Covenant Faith Bible Ministry, with headquarters in Amoyo, Kwara State, and a growing branch in Abeokuta, Ogun State. His ministry spans across Nigeria and beyond, igniting fires of revival, raising men of destiny, and teaching the Word with a precision and anointing that dismantles strongholds and rebuilds lives from the ground up.

A prolific teacher of the Word, Pastor Abioye holds a Doctorate Degree and brings together the precision of scholarship and the fire of the Holy Ghost in every message he delivers. His preaching is marked by deep scriptural revelation, prophetic clarity, and a passionate burden for the Second Coming of Christ, calling believers not just to salvation, but to readiness, consecration, and kingdom productivity.

Through his Ark of God School of Discipleship and College of Mission, he has committed himself to raising not just converts, but commissioned kingdom architects, men and women who carry the weight of eternity in their hearts and the authority of heaven in their steps.

To know him is to encounter a man wholly consumed by God. To sit under his ministry is to find yourself undone and rebuilt for eternity.

Daily Prophetic Declarations

Declare these words over your life. Faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the Word of God.

Declaration 1

I am covered by the blood of Jesus. No weapon formed against me shall prosper. I walk in the covenant blessings of Abraham, and I am blessed going out and blessed coming in, in Jesus name!

Declaration 2

My destiny cannot be buried. My purpose cannot be silenced. My future cannot be stolen. I am God's workmanship, created for good works, and I will fulfil every assignment written about me before the foundation of the world!

Declaration 3

I declare that every dead thing in my life is receiving resurrection power right now. Like Lazarus came forth from the grave, my finances, my health, my marriage, my ministry and my destiny shall come forth in the name of Jesus!

Declaration 4

I am ready for the coming of the Lord. My lamp is full of oil. My life is aligned with eternity. I shall not be found sleeping when the Bridegroom appears. Maranatha! Even so, come Lord Jesus!

Declaration 5

I declare that this nation, Nigeria, shall experience a mighty revival. The Spirit of God is moving across every city and every village. The harvest is great and labourers are rising. The glory of the Lord shall cover this land!

Declaration 6

I am more than a conqueror through Christ who loves me. No principality, no power, no darkness, no opposition can stop what God has ordained for my life. I walk in victory today and every day, in the mighty name of Jesus!

Today's Prophetic Declarations

Father, I thank You that I am covered by the blood of Jesus. Every covenant You made with Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, I am a partaker of that covenant by faith in Jesus Christ!
I decree every dead situation in my life is receiving the resurrection power of the Holy Ghost. Like Lazarus, my finances, health, marriage, and destiny shall come out of every grave!
Lord, I pray for a fresh anointing. Let the oil of the Holy Ghost flow afresh. Anoint my eyes to see, my ears to hear, and my heart to discern the times and seasons of God.
I rebuke every spirit of slumber and spiritual complacency. Lord, wake me up! Give me a burning passion for Your Word and urgency for the harvest. Let me not be caught unprepared at the coming of the Lord.
Father, let Your glory rest upon The Ark of God Covenant Faith Bible Ministry. Let every seed sown here bear a hundredfold return. Let every life that encounters this ministry be transformed forever.
I pray for Nigeria. Lord, raise godly leaders, pour Your Spirit upon this nation, and let a great revival sweep across every state, every village, every campus. Let Nigeria fulfil her prophetic destiny!
Lord Jesus, come quickly. Maranatha. We are watching, we are waiting, we are ready. Come, Lord Jesus. Amen.
